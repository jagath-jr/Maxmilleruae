About Us

Established in 2002, ALUCAD Aluminium & Glass LLC is a leading aluminium and glass company in Abu Dhabi, UAE, specializing in the design, fabrication, and installation of advanced architectural systems. With over two decades of proven expertise, we provide end-to-end solutions for curtain wall systems, glazing works, cladding, doors, windows, frameless glass structures, handrails, and custom architectural metalwork. Our work spans across residential, commercial, hospitality, and high-rise building projects, delivering quality that meets international safety and performance standards. Powered by a dedicated in-house team and cutting-edge CNC and fabrication technology, ALUCAD stands out for its precision craftsmanship, timely project execution, and commitment to customer satisfaction. We collaborate with top-tier suppliers and adhere strictly to ISO-certified quality, health, and safety protocols. From concept to completion, every ALUCAD project reflects innovation, durability, and aesthetic excellence — making us one of the most trusted aluminium and glass contractors in the UAE construction industry.

We take a client-first approach, offering fully customized aluminium and glass solutions tailored to each project's unique requirements. Our proven production cycle ensures efficiency, quality control, and seamless integration at every phase of execution. Whether it's a high-performance façade or intricate architectural glasswork, we aim to exceed expectations through a balance of functionality, sustainability, and design. At ALUCAD, we don’t just build structures — we create lasting impressions through innovation and integrity.
