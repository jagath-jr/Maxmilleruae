Our Services

At ALUCAD Aluminium & Glass LLC, we deliver comprehensive aluminium and glass solutions across the UAE, with expertise in design, fabrication, and installation. Our core services include curtain wall systems, glazing works, aluminium doors and windows, frameless glass structures, ACP and stainless steel cladding, glass balustrades, metal handrails, and architectural features such as skylights, domes, pergolas, and decorative screens. We use advanced machinery and high-quality materials to ensure precision, durability, and compliance with international safety and quality standards. Trusted by clients across residential, commercial, and hospitality sectors, ALUCAD is committed to delivering aesthetically driven and performance-based solutions that meet the evolving needs of modern construction in Abu Dhabi and the UAE.

Aluminium and Glass

ALUCAD Aluminium & Glass LLC delivers premium aluminium and glass solutions across the UAE. We specialize in designing, fabricating, and installing curtain wall systems, unitized façades, structural glazing, and custom aluminium works. Our energy-efficient and weather-resistant solutions enhance the look and strength of commercial buildings, high-rise towers, and luxury homes. Committed to international quality standards, we offer reliable aluminium and glass services in Dubai and beyond, making us a leading aluminium and glass contractor in the region.

Doors and Windows

ALUCAD Aluminium & Glass LLC provides top-quality aluminium doors and windows in the UAE, built for performance, durability, and modern aesthetics. Our range includes sliding, hinged, foldable, and emergency doors, along with casement, top-hung, and horizontal sliding windows. Designed for thermal insulation, smooth operation, and security, our products suit residential, commercial, and industrial needs. With advanced fabrication and premium materials, ALUCAD ensures long-lasting value, making us a trusted aluminium doors and windows supplier in the UAE construction industry.

Cladding Solution

ALUCAD Aluminium & Glass LLC offers expert cladding solutions that combine protection, performance, and visual appeal. We provide aluminium composite panel (ACP) and stainless steel cladding in finishes like mirror, brushed, and anodized. Designed for fire safety, thermal efficiency, and modern aesthetics, our cladding suits high-rises, commercial facades, and architectural features. With precise installation, weatherproof durability, and diverse textures and colors, ALUCAD delivers long-lasting cladding systems trusted across the UAE’s modern construction and architectural landscape.

Frameless Structure

ALUCAD Aluminium & Glass LLC specializes in frameless glass solutions that offer a sleek, modern look for commercial, residential, and hospitality spaces. Our range includes frameless office partitions, shopfronts, and shower enclosures made from high-quality tempered and laminated glass. Designed for transparency, space efficiency, and easy maintenance, we also offer custom finishes like sandblasted glass and decorative films. ALUCAD’s frameless glass structures blend style, safety, and durability, making us a trusted name in minimalist glass designs across the UAE.

Handrails and Balustrades

ALUCAD Aluminium & Glass LLC provides high-quality handrails and balustrades that combine safety, durability, and modern design. Our range includes glass balustrades, stainless steel railings, and aluminium handrails—available in both welded and modular formats. Ideal for staircases, balconies, and ramps, our systems meet top safety standards and offer corrosion resistance with sleek finishes. Designed for residential, commercial, and public spaces, ALUCAD ensures every installation delivers long-lasting performance and a seamless architectural fit across the UAE.

Architectural Feature

ALUCAD Aluminium & Glass LLC designs and installs custom architectural features that enhance both style and functionality. Our offerings include skylights, domes, pergolas, and decorative screens made from high-quality aluminium, glass, and steel. Built for durability and weather resistance, these elements integrate seamlessly with modern and traditional architecture. Ideal for villas, commercial spaces, and public areas, our designs improve lighting, ventilation, and aesthetics. ALUCAD combines structural expertise with creativity to deliver impactful, long-lasting architectural solutions across the UAE.
